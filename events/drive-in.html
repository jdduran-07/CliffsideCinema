Drive-in theater


Howdy, y’all! Whether you're staying with us at the B&B or just passing through, we invite you to experience a night of Texas hospitality with a cinematic twist. Join us at our cliffside drive-in movie theater, where the sunset paints the rugged canyon in golds and reds, creating a backdrop that is cinematic in it's own right.
Public or Private Enjoy the movie from the comforts of your vehicle, or bring a blanket to settle under a starlit Texas sky. Enjoy the show with warm popcorn, a refreshing glass of sweet tea, and the kind of friendly charm that only Texas can offer.
Fun for All You don’t have to be staying overnight to savor this one-of-a-kind experience—come for the movie, stay for the ambiance, and leave with memories as big and bold as the Lone Star State itself!
