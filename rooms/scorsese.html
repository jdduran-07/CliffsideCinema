Scorsese Suite

Step into a world of cinematic luxury with our premier master suite, a space inspired by the grandeur and elegance of classic films. This spacious haven features a stunning four-poster bed with intricate carved details and rich velvet drapes, inviting you to unwind in the lap of luxury. Relax in a plush vintage chaise lounge or immerse yourself in timeless charm at an ornate wooden desk paired with a supple leather chair.

  • The room's decor evokes a sense of old-world sophistication with elegant wall sconces, a curated vintage film poster, and an antique bar cart complete with crystal decanters. The warm, moody lighting sets the perfect ambiance for a memorable evening.
  • What truly sets the Scorsese Suite apart is the breathtaking view through its oversized window. Overlooking the majestic Palo Duro Canyon, guests can marvel at the expansive, moonlit landscape under a canopy of stars. This cinematic setting offers a one-of-a-kind blend of luxury, comfort, and dramatic natural beauty.
  • Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or seeking a peaceful escape, this master suite promises an unforgettable stay.
